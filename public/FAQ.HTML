How does Print this way work?

  1. Login to Print this way webpage
  2. Upload your documents to be printed
  3. Choose from different customisations available - B/W or Colour, Portrait or Landscape
  4. Checkout to place the order
  5. Get your documents at your doorsteps in minutes
  6. Your documents are deleted once printed

What file formats can I print?

You can upload documents in any of the popular file formats like JPG, JPEG, PNG, PDF and many more.

Are my documents stored & handled securely?

Your files are automatically printed at the dark store near you, Safely picked and packed in a sealed envelope by the Store Team and handed over to a delivery partner. Your documents are deleted from our servers once printed.
